**Title:**
John the Ripper Basics – Password Hash Analysis

**Description:**
Practiced the fundamentals of John the Ripper, a widely used password security auditing tool. During this hands-on exercise,
I explored how password hashes can be analyzed in a controlled lab environment to understand the risks of weak passwords. This
practice helped me learn about password hashing, security best practices, and the importance of using strong and unique passwords 
to protect accounts and sensitive information. The activity was conducted for educational and ethical learning purposes only.
