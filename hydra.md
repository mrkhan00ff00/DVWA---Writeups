**Title:**
Hydra Basics – Password Auditing and Authentication Testing

**Description:**
Practiced the fundamentals of Hydra, a tool commonly used in security assessments to evaluate authentication mechanisms.
During this hands-on exercise, I explored how login services can be tested in a controlled lab environment to understand
the risks associated with weak passwords and poor authentication practices. This activity helped strengthen my understanding
of access control, password security, and the importance of implementing strong authentication measures to protect systems from
unauthorized access.
