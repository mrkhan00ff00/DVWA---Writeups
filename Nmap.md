**Title:**
Nmap Basics – Network Discovery and Port Scanning

**Description:**
Practiced the fundamentals of Nmap (Network Mapper), one of the most widely used tools for network reconnaissance 
and security assessment. During this exercise, I explored host discovery, basic port scanning, service detection, 
and network enumeration techniques. Understanding how to identify active hosts, open ports, and running services 
is an essential skill in cybersecurity and penetration testing. This hands-on practice helped strengthen my knowledge
of network mapping and the importance of identifying potential attack surfaces in a controlled lab environment.
